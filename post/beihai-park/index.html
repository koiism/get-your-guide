Beihai Park Ticket Purchase Introduction

Opening Hours

General Area:

  • April 1st - October 31st: 6:00AM- 9:00PM (Last Entry at 8:30PM)
  • November 1st to March 31st: 6:30 AM - 8:00 PM(closed at 7:30PM)

Special Areas (Including the garden and small courtyard within Beihai Park):

  • April 1st to October 31st: 8:00 AM - 6:00 PM (Last Entry at 5:30 PM)
  • November 1st to March 31st: 8:00 AM - 5:00 PM (Last Entry at 4:30 PM)

Ticket Options and Pricing

Regular Admission:

  • Price: $4.50
  • Grants access to the majority of the park's attractions spread over its vast expanse.

Combo Admission:

  • Price: $8.50
  • Includes Regular Admission and access to all special areas and landmarks.

Free Ticket Policy

Visitors aged 60 and above can enter the park for free, including all general and special areas, upon verification of their ID or passport.

Required Visitor Information

Please indicate your preferred visiting date and entry time slot, which can be one of the following:

  • 06:00 AM - 09:00 AM
  • 09:00 AM - 12:00 PM
  • 12:00 PM - 04:00 PM
  • 04:00 PM - 08:30 PM

Moreover, kindly provide the full name, nationality, and ID/passport number for all visitors.

Park Entry Guidelines

You may enter Beihai Park via the designated gates. Please ensure you have your ticket QR code and ID/passport ready for verification upon entry. We look forward to welcoming you to the serene and historic environs of Beihai Park!
